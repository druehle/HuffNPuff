Puffing Wolf

Inspired by a popular “house-building” slot. This is an original, non-commercial recreation with simplified math and visuals.

  • 5×3 reels, 25 lines, wilds substitute.
  • 6+ hats trigger Respins. Hats stick and upgrade Straw → Wood → Brick.
  • After Respins, each house awards credits. Brick can reveal jackpots.
